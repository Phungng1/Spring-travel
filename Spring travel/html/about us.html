Introduction

The Oasis is the largest hotel chain in the world by the number of available rooms. It has 31 brands with 8,000 properties containing 1,423,044 rooms in 139 countries and territories. Of these 8,000 properties, 2,149 are operated by OAS, and 5,493 are operated by others pursuant to franchise agreements. The company also operates 20 hotel reservation centers.
